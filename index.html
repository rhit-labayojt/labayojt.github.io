About

As a third year Computer Science student I find a strange joy in the midst of spreadsheets and analytics. Coming from eastern Illinois to study at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology in Terre Haute, Indiana, my interests lie in data science and website development.

Outside the scope of academics and career, I find my time being taken by pursuits of self-expression and bouts of creativity. Whether it be through writing or digital mediums, I find importance in the reflection and compiling of one's unique experiences and the endless ways storytelling can manifest outside of simple words on paper.
